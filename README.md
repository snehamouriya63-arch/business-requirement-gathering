# Business Requirement Gathering & Stakeholder Analysis

## Problem Statement

An online food delivery app is receiving many customer complaints because orders are being delivered late. Customers are unhappy, ratings are decreasing, and the company is losing users. The company wants to identify the reasons for delays and improve delivery performance.

## Stakeholder Matrix

| Stakeholder | Power | Interest |
|------------|--------|----------|
| CEO | High | High |
| Product Manager | High | High |
| Customers | Low | High |
| Delivery Partners | Medium | High |
| Developers | Medium | High |
| Customer Support Team | Low | High |

## Interview Questions

1. Why are deliveries getting delayed?
2. How often do customers complain?
3. Which locations have the most delays?
4. What challenges do delivery partners face?
5. Are restaurants preparing orders on time?
6. How accurate is the delivery estimate?
7. What improvements do customers want?
8. What business losses occur because of delays?
9. How can tracking be improved?
10. Which issue should be solved first?

## Business Requirement Document (BRD)

### Project Name
Improve Food Delivery Efficiency

### Objective
Reduce delivery delays and improve customer satisfaction.

### Current Situation
- Orders arrive late.
- Customers complain frequently.
- Ratings are decreasing.

### Proposed Solution
- Real-time order tracking.
- Better delivery partner allocation.
- Delay notifications.

### Functional Requirements
- Track order location.
- Send delay alerts.
- Show estimated delivery time.

### Non-Functional Requirements
- App availability should be 99.9%.
- Response time should be under 3 seconds.
- Customer data must be secure.

### Success Criteria
- 50% reduction in delivery delays.
- Higher customer satisfaction.
- Fewer customer complaints.
